Al-Anon Family Groups, which includes Alateen, has been in existence for more than 50 years as a community resource providing support to those affected by someone else's drinking. Today, there are about 30,000 Al-Anon and Alateen groups meeting in 112 countries. Our meetings are anonymous and confidential. There are no dues or fees for membership.
